|| Om ganpataye namaha ||
||   Om Namaha Shivay  ||
||     Jai Shree Ram   ||

He poorna varsha madhe. Aple kartavya paar padel.
Abhyaas karayacha. 
Time kadhayacha.
Awashya hi paap to nhi karna.
Man me ram tan me ram jeevan me ram.
Ram naam ka jap karte karte jiunga ab ageka jiwan.
Om namah shivay , Ram bass ye do mantra hai jiwan me.
